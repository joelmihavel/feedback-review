    Step 01
    You fill the onboarding form
    After expressing interest, you get an email with a link to fill your details — name, ID, preferences, work info.
    📱 What the app does

    Guided digital form — no paperwork, no printing

    • 📝 Single online form collects your Aadhaar, PAN, address, and work details in one go
    • ✅ Form validates and confirms receipt automatically — you always know it went through auto
    👥 Who's working for you

    Sales team handles first contact

    • 📅 Sales shares a calendar link — you pick a property visit slot yourself, no back-and-forth calls
    • 🤝 Once your form is submitted, they guide you to payment and hand you over to CX
    Step 02
    You pay the ₹15,000 token to lock the room
    Your room isn't confirmed until token payment is done. The app makes this instant and transparent.
    📱 What the app does

    Token payment triggers your entire onboarding

    • 🔗 Payment link sent to you on WhatsApp — pay in seconds
    • 📲 Once paid, a QR code is automatically sent to download the Flent app auto
    • 🔔 CX team is notified automatically and your onboarding file is created auto
    • 🏠 Your room is now officially reserved in the system
    👥 Who's working for you

    CX takes over from Sales

    • 💼 CX team is automatically looped in — no manual handoff needed
    • 🧹 CX schedules cleaning and prepares the property before you arrive
    Step 03
    You pay deposit + first rent & get access
    Before move-in day, you complete payments and the app gives you everything you need to walk in.
    📱 What the app does

    Your home is ready on the app before you arrive

    • 🔑 Lockbox access code is released to you once full payment is confirmed auto
    • 📋 All property details — annexures, society rules, utility info — appear on your app
    • 👋 A welcome email with move-in instructions is sent 2–3 days before auto
    • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Your profile card is created and shared with existing flatmates auto
    • 🏘️ Flatmates see who's arriving — cleaning and handover gets coordinated
    👥 Who's working for you

    Three teams ensure move-in day is smooth

    • 💼 CX adds all your property info and populates the digital property file
    • 🏘️ Landlord CX coordinates with your building's society so entry is cleared
    • 🏗️ Partner Success has already handed over keys and set up the property before you arrive
    Every Month
    Paying rent & charges
    Rent is due between the 1st and 5th. The app tells you exactly what you owe and when, so there are no surprises.
    📱 What the app does

    Invoices land before the deadline — every time

    • 🧾 Monthly invoice auto-generated with rent, any extra charges, and due date auto
    • ⚠️ Penalty notice is included so you always know the cost of being late
    • 📩 Reminder sent if payment hasn't come in by the deadline auto
    • 📊 Full payment history visible on your profile at any time
    👥 Who's working for you

    CX monitors and resolves billing queries

    • 💼 CX team adds any additional charges (maintenance dues, etc.) directly from the app
    • 🏘️ Landlord can see monthly costs and payments on their dashboard
    When Something Breaks
    Raising a maintenance issue
    Something's broken or needs fixing. Instead of texting a number and hoping for the best, the app tracks it from start to finish.
    📱 What the app does

    Raise a ticket in seconds — track it in real time

    • 📷 Upload a photo of the issue — the app requires this before submitting, so vendors know exactly what's needed
    • 🔖 Pick the issue category (plumbing, electrical, furniture, etc.) and it routes to the right vendor automatically auto
    • 📍 Live status updates as the ticket moves from "Assigned" to "In Progress" to "Done"
    • ⭐ Once resolved, a quick satisfaction survey is sent to you auto
    👥 Who's working for you

    CX and vendors act on your ticket

    • 💼 CX reviews complex tickets, assigns the right vendor, and follows up
    • 🔧 Vendor receives your photos and issue details, picks a time, and marks work done
    • 🏘️ Landlord can see the issue and how quickly it's being resolved on their dashboard
    Day to Day
    Flatmates & society updates
    Living with others means coordination. The app handles shared dues, notices, and society announcements in one feed.
    📱 What the app does

    All flat communications in one place

    • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Flatmates can log shared dues on the app — you see them as Flent notifications, no WhatsApp arguments
    • 📢 Society notices (maintenance, events, rules) show up on the app — pre-screened by Flent first
    • 🤖 AI chat answers your common questions instantly (policies, charges, society rules) — no need to call anyone new
    👥 Who's working for you

    Flent screens what reaches you

    • ✅ Flemp (Flent monitor) reviews all society notifications before they reach you — only relevant ones come through
    • 🏘️ Landlord can send you messages, but these go through Flent approval first
    Step 01
    You request to move out
    No phone calls, no emails in the dark. You submit your move-out request on the app and everything kicks off from there.
    📱 What the app does

    One request starts the whole exit process new

    • 📤 Dedicated move-out request page on the app — select your date, submit, done
    • 🔔 Flent's CX and contracts teams are notified instantly auto
    • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Your flatmates are notified to settle any shared dues with you before you leave auto
    👥 Who's working for you

    CX confirms and coordinates

    • 💼 CX team verifies your move-out date and schedules an inspection
    • 🏘️ Landlord CX checks if the building society has any exit dues or charges
    Step 02
    Property inspection
    A Flent team member walks through the property. Everything is documented with photos so there's a clear, evidence-backed record.
    📱 What the app does

    Photo-documented inspection — no disputes

    • 📷 Inspection findings are logged on the app with photos during the walkthrough
    • 📋 Every item in the flat is checked against the original inventory logged at move-in
    • 🔗 Report goes directly to the Contracts team — no manual forwarding auto
    👥 Who's working for you

    Product team conducts the inspection

    • 📦 Product team does a thorough walk-through with automated photo documentation
    • 🔎 Checks all inventory items logged during setup — any missing or damaged items are flagged
    Step 03
    Deposit deduction & refund
    Your deposit is refunded after any valid deductions. The app shows you exactly what's being deducted and why — no black box.
    📱 What the app does

    Transparent deductions with photo evidence

    • 💰 Full deduction breakdown shared with you directly on the app — itemised and clear
    • 🖼️ Inspection photos are attached so you can see the evidence behind each deduction
    • 📩 Refund amount communicated to you via the app and email auto
    • 🔄 Refund status is trackable so you know where your money is at all times
    👥 Who's working for you

    Contracts team calculates and processes

    • 📋 Contracts team receives the full inspection report in the app, calculates deductions, and issues refund
    • 💬 They communicate the final amount and any queries through the platform — not over phone

    Replaces: HubSpot + manual sheets
    🎫

    Assign maintenance tickets to vendors, track progress live, and close issues — all in one place. AI handles the simple queries so the team focuses on complex ones.

    Share calendar links with tenants that only show available maintenance slots — no back-and-forth to find a time that works.
